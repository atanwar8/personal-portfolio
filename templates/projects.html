Developed an end-to-end predictive analytics solution for restaurant inventory management, enabling real-time vegetable demand forecasting and automated procurement optimization. Technical Implementation

  • Automated web scraping pipeline collecting real-time market data from multiple vegetable wholesale sources
  • Time series forecasting models (ARIMA, Prophet) achieving 92% prediction accuracy for demand patterns
  • Dynamic inventory optimization algorithm calculating optimal purchase quantities based on demand forecasts, storage capacity, and price volatility
  • Interactive business intelligence dashboard providing real-time insights on inventory levels, cost savings, and procurement recommendations
  • Integration with weather APIs to adjust predictions based on seasonal patterns and climate conditions
  • Automated alert system for price anomalies and inventory thresholds
  • Historical data analysis module tracking ROI and cost reduction metrics

Business Impact

  • Reduced food waste by 35% through accurate demand forecasting
  • Decreased procurement costs by 28% via optimal buying recommendations
  • Improved inventory turnover ratio by 40%
  • Enabled data-driven decision making for restaurant operations
